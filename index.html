What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity means keeping your digital life safe. It's the practice of protecting your devices, information, and identity from digital attacks and unauthorized access—just like locking your house at night! As our world becomes more connected, cybersecurity is essential for everyone—from individuals to businesses and governments.

Your information is the treasure hackers want to steal.
Cybersecurity is your digital shield.
