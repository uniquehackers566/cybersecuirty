Cybersecurity FAQ

  • What is cybersecurity?Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks.
  • Why are strong passwords important?Strong passwords make it harder for attackers to gain unauthorized access to your accounts.
  • What is phishing?Phishing is a type of attack where fake messages trick you into revealing sensitive information.
  • How often should I update my software?Update your software as soon as updates are available to patch security vulnerabilities.
  • What should I do if I suspect a cyber attack?Disconnect from the internet, change your passwords, and contact your IT/security team or a professional.
