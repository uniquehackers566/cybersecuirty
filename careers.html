Career Paths in Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is a rapidly growing field with many career options. Below are some common roles:

🔍

Security Analyst

Monitors networks for security breaches and investigates violations.
Skills: Analysis, threat detection, incident response.

💻

Penetration Tester

Ethical hacker who tries to find vulnerabilities in systems before criminals do.
Skills: Hacking techniques, coding, problem-solving.

🛠️

Security Engineer

Designs and builds secure network and system architectures.
Skills: Network security, software, architecture design.

🔎

Forensic Expert

Investigates security incidents by analyzing digital evidence.
Skills: Forensic tools, attention to detail, legal knowledge.
